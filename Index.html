Quiz Game (A Teachable Machine Website)

Welcome to the quiz game. Hold up your hand in Open Palm to answer Yes. Hold up your hand in a Closed Fist to answer No.

You have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as possible.

Position camera like this for best results.

Lower your hand after you are finished answering
